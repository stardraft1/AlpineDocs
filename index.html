rocket_launchEarly Game Info

Starting out on AlpineSMP is straightforward but exciting. Here’s what you need to know:

• Use /spawn to return to the hub at any time.

• Find a spot at least 500 blocks away from spawn to start your base.

• Use /rtp to teleport to a random location in the wilderness.

• Claim your land early using the golden shovel to prevent griefing.

gavelServer Rules

1. No griefing bases or builds. Stealing is allowed.

2. Follow all new rules and grace periods.

4. No cheating or exploiting.

5. Do not take advantage of Bedrock players.

6. No killing players without armor.
