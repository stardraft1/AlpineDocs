Welcome

This documentation outlines the rules, expectations, plugins, and systems we use. By playing on AlpineSMP you agree to everything listed below.

Server Rules

1. Respect all players

No harassment, hate speech, excessive toxicity, or personal attacks.

2. No cheating or unfair advantages

This includes X-ray, hacked clients, duping, exploiting glitches, or any unfair modifications.

3. No griefing or stealing

Do not destroy, modify, or take items from builds that are not yours without permission.

4. Use common sense

If something gives you an unfair advantage or harms the server experience, do not do it.

5. Staff decisions are final

Staff reserve the right to enforce rules and issue punishments when necessary.
