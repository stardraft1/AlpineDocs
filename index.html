homeWelcome

This handbook outlines the rules, expectations, and systems that keep the server fair, organized, and enjoyable for everyone.

By playing on Alpine SMP, you agree to follow everything listed below. Failure to read this handbook is not an excuse for breaking rules.

rocket_launchEarly Game Information

How to Join: Go to the 'Servers' Tab in Minecraft to add the server using the details found in the Rules popup.

Alt Accounts: It is strictly prohibited to use alt accounts to get more hearts.

Support: If you need support, you can reach out through our WhatsApp Group.

gavelServer Rules

1. No griefing bases or builds. Stealing from other players is allowed.

2. All players must follow new rules and respect grace periods.

3. Respect all players. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying are not allowed.

4. No cheating or exploiting bugs.

5. Do not take advantage of Bedrock players.

6. You may not kill players who have no armor. If a player removes their armor after engaging, they may be attacked.
