gavel Server Rules

1. No griefing bases or builds. Stealing from other players is allowed.

2. All players must follow new rules and respect any announced grace periods.

3. Respect all players. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying are not allowed.

4. No cheating or exploiting bugs.

5. Do not take advantage of Bedrock players.

6. You may not kill players who have no armor. If a player removes their armor after engaging, they may be attacked.

Breaking rules may end in disciplinary action.
